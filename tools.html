Cybersecurity Tools

Protect Yourself from Cybercrime with These Free Tools

VirusTotal

Check to scan your files and URLs for potential security threats. The site analyzes the file or URL using multiple antivirus engines and provides a report on any potential risks. VirusTotal also offers a browser extension to help protect against malicious websites and files.

Check Now
Have I Been Pwned

Check to see if your email address or other personal information has been exposed in known data breaches. The site compiles a database of compromised accounts and allows users to search for their own data to determine if they have been affected. It also offers advice on how to protect your personal information online.

Check Now
No Need to Pay Ransom

Check to see if your files have been compromised by known ransomware threats. The site provides a list of decryption tools that can help recover your files, as well as prevention tips and advice to help protect against future attacks.

Check Now
Password Manager

Check to ensure the security of your online accounts. Dashlane is a password management tool that securely stores and auto-fills your passwords, making it easy to create and maintain strong, unique passwords for each account. It also provides alerts for potential security breaches and offers a VPN for secure browsing on public Wi-Fi

Multi-Factor Authentication

Check to secure your online accounts against unauthorized access. The site offers two-factor authentication tools that can help protect your accounts, as well as real-time alerts that notify you of any suspicious login attempts

ProtonVPN

Check the security of your online activities with ProtonVPN. The service offers a secure and private virtual private network (VPN) that encrypts your internet connection and protects your online privacy. With ProtonVPN, you can browse the internet securely and access blocked content from anywhere in the world.

Avast Antivirus

Use Avast antivirus to protect your computer and personal data from malware and other security threats. The software offers real-time protection, scanning of downloads and email attachments, and the ability to block malicious websites. It also includes a range of additional features, such as a password manager, firewall, and secure browser, to help keep your online activities secure.

Check Now
Cloudfare DDoS Protection

Protect your website from DDoS attacks with Cloudflare's powerful security solutions. Cloudflare offers a suite of tools and services that help detect, mitigate, and prevent DDoS attacks before they can cause damage. Check your website's security status and take advantage of Cloudflare's robust network to keep your online assets safe and available.

Web Firewall

Use Quad9 to enhance your online security by leveraging its free, public Domain Name System (DNS) service. By using Quad9's DNS servers, your internet traffic is automatically filtered for known malicious domains, blocking access to sites that are known to distribute malware, phish for personal information, or engage in other cybercriminal activities.
