What is a CyberCrime?

Cybercrime awareness is the knowledge and understanding of the various types of cyber threats and how to prevent them. By being aware of the risks associated with using technology, individuals and organizations can take steps to protect themselves from cyber attacks. Cybercrime awareness is essential in today's digital age to safeguard personal and sensitive information, prevent financial loss, and maintain online security.
