Identity theft is a type of cybercrime where an individual's personal information is stolen and used for fraudulent purposes, such as making unauthorized purchases, applying for loans, or opening accounts. This can cause significant financial and reputational damage to the victim.
If you suspect that your personal information has been stolen and used without your consent, it is important to report the incident to the authorities and take steps to protect your identity.
